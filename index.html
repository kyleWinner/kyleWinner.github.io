My Creative Process

From your photo to a finished portrait - here's how it works

1

Submit Your Photo

Email me your favorite high-quality photo of your pet. The better the photo, the better the portrait! I'll review it and confirm details.

2

Approval & Deposit

Once approved, I'll send an invoice for a 50% deposit to secure your spot in my queue. Current wait time is 2-3 weeks.

3

Creation & Approval

I'll create your portrait and send a digital proof for your approval. Minor adjustments can be made at this stage.

4

Final Payment

Once approved, I'll send the final invoice for the remaining balance plus any shipping costs.

5

Careful Packaging

Your portrait will be carefully packaged with acid-free materials to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

6

Shipping & Tracking

Your portrait will be shipped with tracking information. Domestic orders typically arrive in 3-5 business days.

Photo Requirements

To ensure the best possible portrait, please provide a high-resolution photo with:

  • Clear, well-lit image (natural light is best)
  • High resolution (minimum 1200x1200 pixels)
  • Sharp focus on the eyes and face
  • The pose/expression you want captured
