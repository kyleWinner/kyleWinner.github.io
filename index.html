Get started

This app uses TransportAPI — a free UK transport data service — to fetch live bus departures near you. You'll need a free account.

1

Visit developer.transportapi.com and register for a free account (30 queries/day — enough for personal use).

2

Once registered, create an app. You'll be given an App ID and an App Key.

3

Paste both below and press Go. Your credentials are stored only in your browser.

Your credentials are saved to localStorage and never leave your device.
