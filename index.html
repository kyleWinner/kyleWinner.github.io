Bee Network Live Departures

Get started

This app uses the TfGM Open Data API to fetch live bus departures. You'll need a free API key to get going.

1
Go to developer.tfgm.com and create a free account.
2
Subscribe to the Bus Stop Schedules product under the Products menu.
3
Copy your Primary Key from your profile, then paste it below and click Go.

Your key is saved in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device.
